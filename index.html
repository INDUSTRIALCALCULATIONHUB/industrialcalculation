Professional Engineering Calculators & Industrial Design Resources

Industrial Calculation Hub is a professional online engineering resource platform providing free calculators, design tools, unit converters, and technical reference articles for engineers, designers, consultants, students, and industrial professionals. The platform is developed to simplify engineering calculations used in mechanical, process, power plant, EPC, HVAC, fabrication, and industrial projects.

Engineering calculations are an important part of equipment selection, system design, fabrication, operation, and troubleshooting. Many engineering professionals require quick verification of flow rates, equipment capacity, dimensions, weights, thermal performance, and material properties during daily project activities. Industrial Calculation Hub provides practical calculation tools that help users perform these engineering evaluations quickly and understand the principles behind them.

The calculators are based on established engineering equations and commonly used industrial practices. Each tool includes calculation methodology, formulas, engineering assumptions, application areas, and limitations wherever applicable. The objective is not only to provide numerical results but also to explain the engineering principles used in the calculation.

Engineering Calculators Available

The website provides a growing collection of engineering tools covering multiple industrial disciplines:

  • Fluid & Piping Engineering: Pipe flow rate, velocity, diameter calculation, Reynolds Number, Bernoulli equation, and pressure-related calculations.
  • Mechanical Equipment Engineering: Pump motor selection, equipment sizing, weight calculations, and fabrication-related tools.
  • Thermal Engineering: Heat conduction, thermal expansion, temperature conversion, and heat transfer calculations.
  • Material Handling Engineering: Hopper angle calculation, silo sizing, bunker capacity estimation, and bulk material storage calculations.
  • Fabrication & Structural Calculations: Duct weight, flange weight, plate weight, and material quantity estimation tools.
  • Engineering Unit Conversion: Pressure, length, area, volume, temperature, and weight conversion utilities.

These tools are useful for engineers working in industries such as power generation, cement manufacturing, steel plants, waste-to-energy facilities, chemical industries, water treatment plants, HVAC systems, and industrial EPC projects.
