Professional Engineering Calculators & Industrial Design Resources

Industrial Calculation Hub is a free online platform developed to help engineers, designers, consultants, project managers, students, and industrial professionals perform accurate engineering calculations quickly and efficiently. The website provides practical engineering tools, unit converters, and technical knowledge articles that support day-to-day engineering work across multiple industries.

Our calculators are designed for real industrial applications including process plants, power plants, cement plants, steel plants, waste-to-energy projects, HVAC systems, material handling systems, boiler auxiliaries, ducting, piping, fabrication, and EPC projects. Every calculator focuses on providing fast, reliable, and easy-to-understand results without requiring complex software installations.

Industrial Calculation Hub offers a growing collection of engineering calculators including pipe flow rate calculations, duct flow calculations, air density, thermal expansion, heat transfer, flange weight, duct weight, hopper valley angle, silo and bunker sizing, pump power estimation, and many more. The website also includes engineering unit converters for pressure, length, area, volume, temperature, and weight to simplify daily engineering calculations.

In addition to calculation tools, the website features engineering knowledge articles that explain important engineering concepts such as Bernoulli's Equation, Reynolds Number, Heat Transfer, Boiler Efficiency, Thermal Expansion, Air Density, Pressure Vessel Design, and other mechanical and process engineering topics. These articles are written in a simple and practical manner to help both experienced engineers and engineering students understand the principles behind the calculations.

Our objective is to build one of the most comprehensive online engineering calculation platforms by continuously adding new industrial calculators, engineering references, fabrication tools, and technical resources. Whether you are preparing engineering designs, reviewing project data, estimating equipment sizes, or learning engineering fundamentals, Industrial Calculation Hub aims to provide accurate, practical, and easy-to-use engineering solutions for your daily work.
