Engineering Note

  • Weight-force conversion is based on standard gravity (g = 9.81 m/s²)
  • 1 kg = 9.81 N
  • 1 kN = 1000 N
  • 1 metric ton = 1000 kg
  • All values are rounded to 2 decimals for engineering accuracy
  • Maintain unit consistency in load and structural calculations
