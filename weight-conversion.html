Engineering Note

  • Force is calculated using F = m × g
  • Standard gravity (g) = 9.81 m/s²
  • 1 kg = 9.81 N
  • 1 kN = 1000 N
  • 1 metric ton = 1000 kg
  • Always maintain SI unit consistency in engineering calculations
