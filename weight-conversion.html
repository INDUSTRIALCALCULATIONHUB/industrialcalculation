Engineering Knowledge

  • Weight-force conversion is based on standard gravity: g = 9.81 m/s² (ISO reference value)
  • 1 kilogram-force (kgf) = 9.81 N under standard gravity conditions
  • 1 kilonewton (kN) = 1000 N (SI derived force unit used in structural design)
  • 1 metric ton (tonne) = 1000 kg mass, commonly used in industrial and logistics engineering
  • 1 pound-force (lbf) = 4.44822 N (imperial force unit used in mechanical systems)
  • Force values vary with local gravitational acceleration; 9.81 m/s² is standard but may differ slightly by location
  • In structural and mechanical design, load calculations must distinguish between mass (kg) and force (N)
  • Engineering safety factors are typically applied on calculated loads before final design approval
