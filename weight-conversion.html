Engineering force and mass unit converter

About Weight Conversion

This tool converts engineering force and mass units using Newton as the base reference system.

Applications

  • Structural load calculations
  • Mechanical engineering design
  • Material testing & analysis

Theory

Weight conversion in engineering is based on Newton’s second law:

F = m × g

Information

  • g = 9.81 m/s² (standard gravity)
  • Force is measured in Newton (N)
  • Mass is measured in kg or tons

Notes

  • Always ensure unit consistency
  • Engineering calculations require SI standard conversion
