Engineering unit conversion for length measurement

Engineering Reference

This tool performs length unit conversions based on SI standards widely used in mechanical, civil, and industrial engineering.

Key Conversions

  • 1 meter = 1000 millimeters
  • 1 meter = 100 centimeters
  • 1 inch = 25.4 millimeters
  • 1 foot = 0.3048 meters
  • 1 yard = 0.9144 meters

Applications

  • Engineering design calculations
  • Fabrication and machining
  • Piping and structural layout
  • Drawing interpretation (mm/inch systems)
