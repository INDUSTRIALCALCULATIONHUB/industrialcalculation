The information we collect is used to improve website performance, maintain security, analyze visitor trends, enhance user experience, respond to enquiries, and display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense where applicable.

Industrial Calculation Hub uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies to provide personalized or non-personalized advertisements based on your browsing activity. Users can manage advertising preferences through Google's Ads Settings.

Consent

By using this website, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
