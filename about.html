Who We Are

Industrial Calculation Hub is a free online engineering platform developed to provide practical engineering calculators, unit converters, and technical resources for engineers, consultants, students, and industrial professionals. Our goal is to simplify complex engineering calculations by offering reliable, easy-to-use tools that save time and improve productivity.

The website focuses on real industrial applications across mechanical engineering, process engineering, HVAC systems, power plants, fabrication, piping, material handling, boilers, and EPC projects. We continuously expand our collection of engineering tools and technical articles to support engineers throughout design, estimation, execution, and maintenance activities.

What We Offer

Industrial Calculation Hub provides a growing collection of engineering calculators, industrial design tools, fabrication utilities, engineering unit converters, and technical knowledge articles. Our tools support practical engineering calculations including fluid flow, heat transfer, material properties, pump sizing, thermal expansion, fabrication estimation, and industrial process design.
