Plate or Sheet Weight

Calculate weight of plates or sheets

Engineering Knowledge

Plate weight is calculated using the relation between volume and material density. The volume is obtained from surface area multiplied by thickness.

Basic Formula:

Weight = Area × Thickness × Density

Symbol Description Unit
Area Surface Area of Plate m²
Thickness Plate Thickness m
Density Material Density kg/m³
Weight Total Plate Mass kg

Formulas Used

  • Rectangular Plate Area: A = L × W
  • Circular Plate Area: A = π × D² / 4
  • Volume: V = A × t
  • Weight: W = V × ρ
