Calculate Linear Thermal Expansion, Dimensional Change & Expansion of Engineering Materials Due to Temperature Variation

Engineering Knowledge

Thermal expansion occurs when a material changes its dimensions due to temperature change.

Linear Expansion Formula:

ΔL = α × L₀ × ΔT

Symbol Description Unit
L₀ Initial Length mm or m
ΔT Temperature Change °C
α Coefficient of Expansion /°C
ΔL Change in Length mm or m
