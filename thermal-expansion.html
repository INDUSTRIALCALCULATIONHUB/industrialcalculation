Professional Thermal Expansion Calculator

This professional thermal expansion calculator is designed to estimate the linear expansion of engineering materials when subjected to temperature changes. It is widely used in mechanical, civil, and process engineering applications where dimensional changes due to heat must be considered.

When materials are exposed to temperature variations, they expand or contract based on their coefficient of thermal expansion. This tool calculates the change in length using standard engineering formulas and material-specific coefficients.

Formula Used

Thermal Expansion (ΔL) = α × L × ΔT

  • α = Coefficient of thermal expansion (per °C)
  • L = Original length (mm)
  • ΔT = Temperature difference (°C)

Materials Covered

This calculator includes commonly used industrial materials such as aluminium, carbon steel, stainless steel, copper, brass, bronze, and cast iron, each with standard thermal expansion coefficients.

  • Structural engineering calculations
  • Machinery and equipment design
