Boiler Efficiency Explained

Understanding how industrial boilers convert fuel energy into useful steam energy.

What is Boiler Efficiency?

Boiler efficiency is the ratio of useful heat output to the heat input from fuel combustion. It indicates how effectively a boiler converts fuel energy into steam energy.

Boiler Efficiency Formula

Efficiency (%) = (Heat Output / Heat Input) × 100

  • Heat Output = Energy in generated steam
  • Heat Input = Energy from fuel combustion

Types of Boiler Losses

  • Flue gas loss
  • Radiation loss
  • Unburnt fuel loss
  • Blowdown loss

Factors Affecting Efficiency

  • Fuel quality
  • Excess air ratio
  • Heat transfer surface condition
  • Load variation

Industrial Applications

  • Power plants
  • Refineries
  • Chemical industries
  • Food processing plants
