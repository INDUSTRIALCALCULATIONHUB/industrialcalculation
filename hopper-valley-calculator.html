Engineering Formula

Side Plate Angles:

θL = tan⁻¹(H / (ΔL/2))

θW = tan⁻¹(H / (ΔW/2))

Valley Angle:

θV = tan⁻¹(H / √((ΔL/2)² + (ΔW/2)²))
