About Pipe Flow Rate

Pipe flow rate is the volume of fluid passing through a pipe per unit time. It is widely used in hydraulics, water supply systems, and industrial pipelines.

Formula

Q = A × V

  • Q = Flow rate (m³/s)
  • A = Cross-sectional area (m²)
  • V = Velocity (m/s)

A = π × D² / 4
