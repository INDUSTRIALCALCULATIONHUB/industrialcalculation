About Pipe Flow

Pipe flow rate is the volume of fluid passing through a pipe per unit time. It is widely used in fluid mechanics, HVAC, and process engineering.

Formula

Q = A × V

Where:

  • Q = Flow Rate (m³/s)
  • A = Cross-sectional Area (m²)
  • V = Velocity (m/s)

Applications

  • Piping systems design
  • Water supply engineering
  • Oil & gas pipelines
  • HVAC systems
