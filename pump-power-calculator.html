Pump Power Calculator

Hydraulic power calculation with fluid selection

Formula

P = (ρ × g × Q × H) / η

  • ρ = Fluid density (kg/m³)
  • Q = Flow rate (m³/s)
  • H = Head (m)
  • η = Efficiency
