About Pump Power

Pump power is the energy required to move fluid through a system against gravity and resistance. It is widely used in hydraulic, water supply, and industrial systems.

Formula

P = (ρ × g × Q × H) / η

  • P = Power (Watts)
  • ρ = Density of fluid (kg/m³)
  • g = Gravity (9.81 m/s²)
  • Q = Flow rate (m³/s)
  • H = Head (m)
  • η = Efficiency

Applications

  • Water pumping systems
  • Industrial fluid transfer
  • Irrigation systems
  • Oil & gas pipelines
