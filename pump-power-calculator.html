Pump Power Calculator

Hydraulic power calculation using fluid density

Formula

P = (ρ × g × Q × H) / η

  • ρ = Fluid density (kg/m³)
  • Q = Flow rate (converted to m³/s)
  • H = Head (m)
  • η = Efficiency

Engineering Notes

This tool converts flow rate from m³/hr to m³/s automatically for correct hydraulic calculation.
