Pump Power Calculator

Industrial Pump Design: Hydraulic Power + Motor Selection

Engineering Formula

Hydraulic Power:

Ph = (ρ × g × Q × H) / (3.6 × 10⁶)

Shaft Power:

Ps = Ph / ηpump

Motor Power:

Pm = Ps / ηmotor

Standard Motor Selection

Motor is selected from nearest higher standard rating.
