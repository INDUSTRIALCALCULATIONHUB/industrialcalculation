Engineering pressure unit conversion

Engineering Reference

Pressure conversion is based on SI units using Pascal (Pa) as the base unit. Accurate conversion is essential in mechanical, process, and fluid engineering applications.

Key Conversions

  • 1 bar = 100,000 Pa
  • 1 atm = 101,325 Pa
  • 1 psi = 6,894.76 Pa
  • 1 mmWC = 9.80665 Pa

Applications

  • Piping and duct system design
  • Boiler and pressure vessel calculations
  • HVAC and airflow systems
  • Pump and compressor selection
