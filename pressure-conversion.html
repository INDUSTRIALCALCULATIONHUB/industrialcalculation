Engineering Note

  • All conversions are based on SI unit (Pascal)
  • 1 bar = 100,000 Pa
  • 1 atm = 101,325 Pa
  • 1 psi = 6,894.76 Pa
  • 1 mmWC = 9.80665 Pa
  • Values are rounded to 2 decimals
  • Use consistent units in fluid mechanics calculations
