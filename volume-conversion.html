Engineering Knowledge

Fundamental Basis

  • Volume measurements are derived from the SI base unit: cubic meter (m³)
  • All conversions are standardized through SI traceable relationships
  • Used as a primary reference in fluid mechanics and process engineering calculations
  • Ensures dimensional consistency in storage, flow, and system design applications

Standard Conversion Factors

  • 1 cubic meter (m³) = 1000 liters (L)
  • 1 cubic foot (ft³) = 0.0283168 m³
  • 1 cubic inch (in³) = 0.0000163871 m³
  • 1 US gallon = 0.00378541 m³

Engineering Application Areas

  • Fluid system design and pipeline capacity calculations
  • Tank, vessel, and reservoir sizing
  • Chemical and process industry mass balance calculations
  • HVAC, hydraulic, and thermal system design
