Engineering Knowledge

Volume is a three-dimensional physical quantity representing space occupied by a substance, expressed in cubic units (L³). It is a fundamental parameter in fluid mechanics, process design, and storage system engineering.

Fundamental Basis

  • Volume is derived from SI base unit: cubic meter (m³)
  • It represents L³ dimensional physical quantity
  • All conversions are based on internationally accepted SI standards
  • Ensures consistency in hydraulic, thermal, and process systems

Standard Conversion Factors

  • 1 m³ = 1000 litres
  • 1 ft³ = 0.0283168 m³
  • 1 in³ = 0.0000163871 m³
  • 1 US gallon = 0.00378541 m³

Engineering Application Areas

  • Pipeline flow and hydraulic system design
  • Tank, vessel, and reservoir sizing
  • Chemical process and batch calculation systems
  • HVAC and fluid transport engineering
  • Storage and logistics volume estimation
