Engineering Knowledge

Volume is a derived physical quantity representing three-dimensional space (L³), used extensively in fluid mechanics, thermodynamics, and process engineering.

Fundamental Basis

  • SI base unit: cubic meter (m³)
  • Derived from length cubed (L³ dimensional form)
  • Standardized using internationally accepted SI constants
  • Ensures consistency in engineering calculations

Standard Conversion Factors

  • 1 m³ = 1000 liters
  • 1 ft³ = 0.0283168 m³
  • 1 in³ = 0.0000163871 m³
  • 1 US gallon = 0.00378541 m³

Engineering Applications

  • Pipeline and fluid transport system design
  • Tank, vessel, and reservoir sizing
  • Process engineering mass balance calculations
  • HVAC and hydraulic system design
