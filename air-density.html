About Air Density

Air density is the mass of air per unit volume. It changes with altitude, pressure, and temperature and is widely used in engineering applications.

Formula

ρ = P / (R × T)

Applications

  • Aerodynamics
  • HVAC systems
  • Weather studies
  • Industrial design
