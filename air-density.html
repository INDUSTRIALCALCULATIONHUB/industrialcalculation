Air Density Calculator

Calculate air density using altitude and temperature based on engineering atmospheric model.

Theory

Air density is mass per unit volume of air and varies with temperature and pressure.

Information

  • Higher temperature reduces density
  • Higher pressure increases density
  • Used in HVAC, aerodynamics and fluid flow

Notes

  • Standard density at sea level: 1.225 kg/m³
  • Based on ideal gas law
