Air Density Calculator

Calculate air density based on altitude and temperature using standard atmospheric relations. Applicable for fan selection, HVAC systems, and process engineering calculations.

Enter altitude (m) and temperature (°C)

Engineering Basis

  • ρ = P / (R × T)
  • Pressure varies with altitude (ISA model)
  • Temperature decreases with altitude
  • Valid for 0–11 km standard atmosphere

Standard Condition

  • Density: 1.225 kg/m³
  • Temperature: 15°C
  • Pressure: 1 atm

Engineering Applications

  • Fan and blower performance correction
  • Duct airflow and pressure calculations
  • Combustion air requirement
  • HVAC system design
  • Air pollution control systems

Engineering Note

  • Flow ≈ constant
  • Pressure ∝ Density
  • Power ∝ Density
